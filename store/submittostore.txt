How to publish PageTurn to the Chrome Web Store
================================================

1. Go to https://chrome.google.com/webstore/devconsole ($5 one-time fee)

2. Click "New Item" → upload store/pageturn-extension.zip

3. Fill in listing from store/listing.txt (short description + detailed description)

4. Upload the two screenshot PNGs (store/screenshot1.png and store/screenshot2.png)

5. For privacy policy URL, enable GitHub Pages on the repo
   (Settings → Pages → Deploy from branch → master)
   then link to: https://funkypitt.github.io/pageturn/store/privacy-policy.html
   Submit for review — usually approved within 1-3 business days.

PageTurn — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Summary

PageTurn does not collect, store, transmit, or share any data. Period.

What PageTurn does

PageTurn is a Chrome extension that reformats the content of web pages you are currently viewing into a paginated reading layout. All processing happens entirely within your browser tab. No data leaves your device.

Data collection

PageTurn collects no data of any kind. Specifically:

  • No personal information
  • No browsing history
  • No page content
  • No cookies or tracking
  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No network requests — the extension makes zero HTTP calls

Permissions

PageTurn requests two permissions:

  • activeTab — to access the content of the tab you activate it on (only when you click the icon or press Alt+P)
  • scripting — to inject the pagination script into the active tab

These permissions are used solely to reformat the visible page for reading. No data from the page is stored or transmitted anywhere.

Third parties

PageTurn uses no third-party services, libraries, SDKs, or analytics tools.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.

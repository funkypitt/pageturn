SHORT DESCRIPTION (132 chars max):
Turn any article into paginated pages. Instant page turns with arrow keys — no scrolling, no animation. Perfect for e-ink displays.

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:

PageTurn converts scrollable web pages into clean, paginated reading — like a book. Press arrow keys or swipe to flip between pages instantly, with zero animation or scrolling. Ideal for e-ink tablets, but great on any screen.

HOW IT WORKS
• Click the PageTurn icon or press Alt+P on any article
• The page content is extracted and reflowed into screen-sized pages
• Navigate with arrow keys, Space, or swipe gestures
• Press Escape or tap ✕ to return to the original page

OPTIMIZED FOR BLOGS
Automatically detects article content on Substack, Medium, WordPress, Ghost, Dev.to, Hashnode, Blogger, and more. Strips navbars, sidebars, ads, and subscribe prompts — just the article text.

WHY PAGETURN?
• No animation — instant page swap, critical for e-ink refresh rates
• Clean typography — serif font, generous line height, comfortable margins
• Page counter — always know where you are (e.g. "3 / 12")
• Keyboard + touch — arrow keys, Space, swipe, or tap screen edges
• Adapts to any window size — resize or rotate and keep your place
• Zero data collection — everything runs locally, no network requests

KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS
→ or Space: next page
← or Shift+Space: previous page
Home / End: first / last page
Escape: exit reading mode
Alt+P: toggle PageTurn
